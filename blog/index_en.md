# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-feather-pointed" %}Blog

Here we share our research group's academic achievements, practical reflections, and team updates. The content generally falls into three categories: first, in-depth literature reviews, including notes on cutting-edge papers, summaries of phased results, and interpretations of key data to clearly present the research trajectory; second, reflections and practices, recording members' research experiences, problem deductions, cross-disciplinary observations, and trial-and-error processes with an emphasis on logic and empirical sense; third, team dynamics, providing timely updates on project progress, conference activities, and member honors. We write about real problems and actual situations in a plain style to preserve research footprints and provide references for peers.
